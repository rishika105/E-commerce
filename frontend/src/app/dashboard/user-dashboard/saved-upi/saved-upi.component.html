FAQs

What is UPI and how does it work?

UPI (Unified Payments Interface) is a real-time payment system that allows you to instantly transfer money between two bank accounts using a mobile phone. It works by linking your bank account to your UPI ID.

Is it safe to save my UPI ID for future transactions?

Yes, saving your UPI ID is secure. We use encryption to protect your UPI information, ensuring it is safely stored for faster future payments.

Can I update or remove my saved UPI ID later?

Yes, you can update or remove your saved UPI ID in the payment settings. Simply access the saved UPI section and make the necessary changes.

What happens if I don't save my UPI ID during a transaction?

If you don't save your UPI ID during a transaction, you'll need to manually enter it each time you make a payment. You can choose to save it during any future payment process.
